# TRASON OS - User Manual

Welcome to **TRASON OS**, your personal command center. This manual will guide you through the core features and how to effectively manage your life using the platform.

---

## 1. Introduction
TRASON OS is an integrated operating system designed to replace fragmented tools. Instead of using separate apps for finance, habits, and career tracking, TRASON brings everything into a single, cohesive dashboard that connects your daily actions to a holistic "Life Score."

## 2. Getting Started
### Initialization
1. **Sign Up**: Start by creating your account at the initialization screen (`/signup`).
2. **Dashboard**: Upon login, you will land on the **Command Center** (`/dashboard`). This is your daily overview.

---

## 3. The Command Center (Dashboard)
The dashboard provides a high-level summary of your current status.
* **Life Score**: A holistic score (0-100) calculated based on your performance across all modules (Finance, Vitality, Career).
* **Daily Insights**: TRASON automatically analyzes your recent data and provides AI-driven text insights, helping you see correlations (e.g., "High stress correlates with higher spending this week").
* **Quick Add**: Use the universal `+` button to quickly log a financial transaction, a habit completion, or a new job application without leaving the page.

---

## 4. Modules

### 4.1. Financial Flow (`/finance`)
Manage your capital and cash flow.
* **Transactions**: Log income, expenses, and investments.
* **Burn Rate & Runway**: TRASON calculates your monthly burn rate and predicts how long your liquid capital will last.
* **Categories**: Transactions are automatically categorized, allowing you to see exactly where your money goes.

### 4.2. Vitality & Habits (`/sport` / `/habits`)
Build consistency without the guilt.
* **Heatmaps**: View a GitHub-style activity heatmap to track your consistency over the last 30/90 days.
* **Recovery Focus**: If you break a streak, the system encourages "recovery" rather than punishing you with red alerts.
* **Activity Logging**: Log workouts, meditation, reading, or any custom habit.

### 4.3. Career Architect (`/career`)
Stop losing track of job applications.
* **Kanban Pipeline**: Move applications through stages: *Applied*, *Interview*, *Offer*, *Rejected*.
* **Interview Prep**: Attach notes, company research, and salary expectations to each application card.
* **Timeline**: View your career progression and application history chronologically.

### 4.4. Signal Reminders (`/reminders`)
A smart reminder system designed to reduce noise.
* **Context-Aware**: Set reminders that trigger based on time or specific conditions.
* **Snooze with Intelligence**: Push reminders back easily without cluttering your immediate view.

---

## 5. Settings & Privacy
Access your configuration via `/settings`.
* **Privacy First**: Your data is yours. TRASON does not sell your data or use tracking algorithms.
* **Customization**: Adjust your target metrics, default currency, and notification preferences.
* **Data Export**: (Coming Soon) Export all your raw data to a CSV file.
